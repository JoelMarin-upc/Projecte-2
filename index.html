About the Game

What Remains of You is a turn-based RPG where players explore handcrafted levels filled with undead and enemy soldiers. Between expeditions, you return to town to upgrade gear, recover, and hire companions for future battles.

Built in C++ with a custom engine using SDL3 and Box2D.

Features

  • Turn-based combat system with tactical depth
  • Exploration of handcrafted dungeon maps
  • Gear, inventory and quests
  • Companion recruitment and progression
  • Pixel art animations and atmospheric storytelling
  • Gamepad and keyboard support
