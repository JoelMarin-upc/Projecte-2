# What Remains of You

This game is a turn-based RPG where players explore handcrafted levels filled with undead and enemy soldiers. Between expeditions, you return to town to upgrade gear, recover, and hire companions for future battles.

The game is built in **C++** using a custom engine powered by **SDL3** for rendering and **Box2D** for physics.

---

## Core Features

- Turn-based combat system
- Zombie and soldier enemies
- Town hub with shops and companion hiring
- Multiple progression-based levels
- Infection system
- Dynamic light & vision mechanic
- Equipment and party progression

---

## Infection System

Throughout your journey, both you and your companions can become infected by the plague. As infection levels rise, characters gain evolving modifiers that provide **both positive and negative effects**, creating a constant risk-reward balance. Higher infection may grant powerful bonuses, but at increasing danger. If a companion reaches maximum infection, they will turn into a zombie. If the player reaches maximum infection, it results in game over.

Managing infection is crucial to survival.

---

## Light & Vision System

Darkness plays a key gameplay role.

- Your character has a limited field of vision.
- Light sources (such as torches) determine how much of the environment you can see.
- Enemies hidden in darkness remain unseen until illuminated.
- Managing light is essential for survival.

---

## Combat System

Combat is fully turn-based:

- Decide your actions strategically
- Manage health and equipment
- Coordinate your hired companions

Planning and tactics are more important than reaction speed.

---

## Town Hub

After each level, return to town to:

- Purchase weapons and armor
- Rest and recover
- Hire companions
- Prepare for harder encounters

Each expedition becomes more dangerous than the last.
